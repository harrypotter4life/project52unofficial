Interesting Facts about Camels!

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.

Interesting Facts about Chameleons!

  1. Unlike many lizards, chameleons can't regrow their tails.
  2. Chameleons can move their eyes separately, with each eye having a field of 180 degrees.
  3. Chameleons have extremely powerful tongues.

Interesting Facts about Cicadas!

  • Cicadas are one of the loudest insects that we know of.
  • Cicadas get easily confused.
  • Cicadas shed their skin.

Interesting Facts about Pandas!

  1. Pandas have an extended wrist bone that they use like a thumb to help them grip food.
  2. Giant pandas spend 10-16 hours a day feeding, mainly on bamboo.
  3. On average, pandas poo 40 times a day.

Interesting Facts about Pigeons!

  • Pigeons can recognise each letter of the human alphabet, differentiate between photographs, and even distinguish different humans within a photograph.
  • Pigeons will often be seen in flocks of 20-30 birds.
  • Pigeons are spiritual to some religions.

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bears!

  1. Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902
  2. The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  3. After the Titanic sank in 1912, German toy company Steiff created 500 teddy bears to honor the victims.
